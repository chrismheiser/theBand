    About Puppy Pendulum

    Puppy Pendulum is a punk rock band from the cold, desolate Seattle, Washington. They started out as a group of high school friends that ran out of things to do in the summer of 2005. Since it rained every day that year, they often chose not to leave the house, and started learning how to play instruments in Brandon's garage. They played loudly. They played badly. The many months of practice finally started to form into sounds that resembled a punk rock style.

    The band released their first EP, "Throw us a Bone", and began playing small shows around the city. Puppy Pendulum couldn't gain popularity within their hometown, and by summer 2007 they began a tour on their own down the west coast. Their big break came in L.A. after a opening band dropped out and they were able to open for Rise Against.

    Chris Heiser

    My name is Chris. I am the guy that plays all the hipster instruments that you've probably never heard of. Between snowboarding and making some rad tunes with my band, there's nothing else that really matters. I enjoy playing cowbell, any type of shaker (maraca, shaker eggs), and the triangle the most out of all instruments.

    Eduardo Iniguez

    My name is Eduardo Iniguez and I am a member of the Puppy Pendulum musical group. I have been part of this band for several years and have enjoyed it dearly. I play the Drums and in some occasions I also play the Guitar. I enjoy playing sports on my free time as well as develop web pages.
